Republic of Korea

The Republic of Korea is also more commonly known as South Korea. What is probably the most noticeable thing about the Republic of Korea lately is their large musical influence around the world.

The Republic of Korea has many famous companies that people know today. One of which would be Samsung, which started as a typical grocery chain before changing gears into the electronic, construction, and shipbuilding powerhouse that it has become today. 1

Music

K-pop has taken over the world within the last few years. The latest have been shown as the most popular male idol group, BTS, coming to the US for the music premier. They have also hit the top charts within the US. Many people world wide have listened to their songs. There can be many things said about them being a leading group in not only music, choreography, and style.

BTS: #1 Idol Group

BTS is a group that started in 2010, and hit the top charts since their debut. Alway bringins something new and fresh. The members are: Suga, RM, J-Hope, Jimin, V, Jin, and Jungkook

SuperM

This is a recent group that has been formed as another group of 7, however the most noticable are 4 members that have come from other groups. Those groups being: EXO, WayV, NCT 127 and SHINee!

BIGBANG!

Dubbed the "King of Pop", they formed in 2016. They are known for being the starting force of spreading the KPOP genre into the international world. The group contains: G-DRAGON, T.O.P, Taeyang, and Daesung with former member Seungri have retired recently.

Girls' Generation

Girls' Generation is an old favorite featuring 8 members: Taeyeon, Sunny, Tiffany, Hyoyeon, Yuri, Sooyoung, Yoona, Seohyun. A great deal of the inspiration for their songs comes from the gold old oldies: 70s, 80s

Blackpink

An all female group that consists of: Jisoo, Jennie, Rosé, and Lisa. The group of girls are all from Seoul, South Korea. They were the first K-Pop group to join Youtube's "Billion Views Club" with their single "Ddu-Du Ddu-Du". The only other KPOP songs you may have seen on that list is Psy's Gangnam Style!

EXO

A popular group that has scored 4 No 1s on World Billboards. They were created in 2012 and were invited to perform in the 2018 Winter Olypmics. Their flavor of music is a mix of: Hip-hop, pop, and R&B. They were labeled as "Biggest Boy band in the World" by numerous media outlets from 2014-2018.
